Mongolian Beef

Mongolian Beef Recipe


This recipe is not the easiest but it super delisious! You have to try it when you get a chance.


Ingredients

    ▢8 ounces flank steak (225g, sliced against the grain into 1/4-inch thick slices) ▢1 teaspoon vegetable oil (plus 1/3 cup for frying) ▢1 teaspoon soy sauce ▢1 tablespoon cornstarch (plus 1/4 cup, divided) ▢2 tablespoons brown sugar ▢1/4 cup hot water (or hot low sodium chicken or beef stock) ▢1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce or 1 1/2 tablespoons water and 2 1/2 tablespoons regular soy sauce ▢1/2 teaspoon ginger (minced) ▢5 dried red chili peppers (optional) ▢2 cloves garlic (chopped) ▢1 tablespoon cornstarch (mixed with 1 tablespoon water to make a slurry) ▢2 scallions (cut into 1-inch long slices on the diagonal)
